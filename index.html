About Me

I'm a game designer and programmer with three years of professional experience.

In the past few years, I've been focusing on feature design, narrative design, and programming.
I'm constantly seeking inspiration from other types of media like movies, animation, and comics. Which is why I try to consume as much diverse media as possible to expand my cultural palette and worldview.
